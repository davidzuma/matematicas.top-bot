Matematicas.top Bot Demo

matematicas.top-bot helps you solve mathematical problems and answer questions. You can send it equations or images with mathematical problems, and the bot will solve them using artificial intelligence. The bot is also backed by reference videos from the Matematicas.top YouTube channel.

Bot Features

  • Solves mathematical problems and provide you with example of videos from Matematicas.top YouTube channel.

  • Use SQLite Cloud with vector extension for RAG and user and cost tracking
  • AI Agent that uses vision capabilities of OpenAI's GPT-4o model and routing to answer effectively mathematical question with the most relevant information
  • Includes referral program and token tracking
Warning: Answers may not be 100% accurate. By using this bot, you agree that messages and images are sent to an external AI provider for processing. Do not send sensitive information.
