Matematicas.top Bot Demo

matematicas.top-bot helps you solve mathematical problems and answer questions. You can send it equations or images with mathematical problems, and the bot will solve them using artificial intelligence.

Bot Features

  • Solves mathematical problems
  • Processes written equations and equations in images
  • Uses advanced artificial intelligence
  • Implements RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) with SQLite Cloud and vectors
  • Uses vision capabilities of OpenAI's GPT-4o model
Warning: Answers may not be 100% accurate. By using this bot, you agree that messages and images are sent to an external AI provider for processing. Do not send sensitive information.

How it Works

The bot uses a combination of advanced technologies to process and respond to your mathematical queries:

  1. RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) with SQLite Cloud and vectors to improve the accuracy of responses.
  2. Artificial vision capabilities of OpenAI's GPT-4o model to interpret images of mathematical problems.
  3. Natural language processing to understand and solve written equations and problems.

This combination of technologies allows the bot to offer precise and detailed solutions to a wide range of mathematical problems.
